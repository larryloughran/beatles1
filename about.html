John Lennon

John Lennon was born on October 9, 1940, in Liverpool, England. He met Paul McCartney in 1957 and invited McCartney to join his band.Lennon left the Beatles in 1969 and later released albums with his wife, Yoko Ono, among others.Lennon/McCartney are still the most successful songwrting partnerships ever.

Paul McCartney

Paul McCartney (born 18 June 1942) is an English singer/songwriter and a key member of the Beatles. After the Beatles split up in 1970, he pursued his own successful solo career, recording with a variety of artists, including his group ‘Wings’. According to the Guinness Book of Records, he is the best selling artist of all time.

Ringo Starr

Ringo Starr is the drummer with the BeaTles.Born in 1940 he joined in 1960.He was never regarded as a tchnically perfect drummer,but was the foundation for the rythm section along with McCartney's bass prowess.He also went on to have a hugely succesful solo career after the beatles split in 1970.

George Harrison

Sometimes regarded as 'The Shy One',George liked to stay in the background and leave the limelight to John and Paul.He was an accomplished guitarist and tailored that classic beatles sound as the band progressed.He had a global hit with 'Got My Mind Set On You' as part of a very successful solo career.
